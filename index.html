📂 Vulnerability Management 📡 NVD

Als we niets doen: Attackers can steal customer authentication tokens, access sensitive policyholder or claims data in real-time, impersonate users, and move laterally through our systems to reach core financial and underwriting systems.

Impact Level Detail

This creates both direct regulatory exposure and indirect revenue risk.

✅ Recommended action: Immediately audit all production instances of n8n for affected versions; prioritize upgrading to 2.25.7 or 2.26.2 this week, and disable HTTP transport mode on any n8n deployment handling policyholder or financial data until patched.
