Impact Level Detail

🏢 Organisational 🔴 Critical Compromise of the AI gateway could disrupt or disable AI-powered claims processing, pricing engines, and underwriting systems, halting critical business operations. Attackers could manipulate decision-making algorithms, leading to incorrect claims payouts or policy approvals.
💻 IT 🔴 Critical This vulnerability is actively exploitable with a 9.8 severity rating and no known public exploit delays; patching to version 1.84.0 or later is required immediately. Any LiteLLM instance not updated is exposed to remote compromise.
✅ Recommended action: Immediately audit all LiteLLM deployments across the enterprise, upgrade to version 1.84.0 or later by end of week, and temporarily isolate any instances that cannot be patched from production networks.

Impact Level Detail
💰 Financial 🔴 Critical Extended service outages could trigger SLA breach penalties, customer compensation claims, and regulatory fines. Reputational damage from unavailable digital channels directly impacts premium renewals and customer retention in a competitive market.

💻 IT 🔴 Critical Patch is available now (versions 2.5.301 and 3.1.7); delay increases exploitation likelihood as attacker toolkits are likely under development. This library is typically embedded in multiple applications, requiring coordinated updates across the portfolio.
✅ Recommended action: Issue emergency patch directive this week requiring all development teams to update MessagePack library to fixed versions and test in staging by end of month; prioritize customer-facing APIs first.
